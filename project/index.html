DIOCLEI COELHO

Once upon a time, I always left the hair salon feeling frustrated. No matter how clearly I explained what I wanted, the hairdressers seemed to have their own ideas. So, I decided to try cutting my own hair. Surprisingly, I found that I really enjoyed it! That frustration turned into a passion for hairstyling. Now, I love listening to what people want and making their hair look great.
